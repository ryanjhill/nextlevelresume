Why hire a resume consultant?

Scoring an interview for your next career can be challenging, largely because it is hard to stand out in a sea of applicants. It's even harder if you don't know what recruiters or computer filters are looking for in your application.

Our professionals know this system inside and out, giving you insights you need to succeed.

What does Next Level do?

Next Level works with customers on a personable level, coaching and working through rewrites as a team. We try to provide enough value and insight that everyone we works with won't need us anymore, and that they'll become their own personal resume experts!
